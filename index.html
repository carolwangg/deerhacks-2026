FORGEAI

SCAN YOUR WORLDOWN IT IN 3D

Point your camera at any real object, spin it slowly, and ForgeAI reconstructs it as an interactive 3D model — powered by a 3-step Gemini Vision chain for maximum accuracy.

01
📸
Capture
Hold your object in front of the camera and capture 5 angles as you slowly rotate it.
02
🧠
Detect
Gemini Vision identifies the object with confidence score — confirming what it sees before deep analysis.
03
🔬
Analyze
Full product identification: material, size, colour, brand, model number, and confidence score.
04
🔗
Match
Gemini finds the best online 3D model reference and gives 5 specific instructions to improve accuracy.
05
⚡
Render
Three.js renders the model using all gathered intelligence, with voice control to refine it further.
